Our Foundation

A Legacy Built on Trust & Technology

Founded in 2000, Current-Link Hospitality Systems was established with a singular vision: to bring world-class technology to Nigeria's burgeoning hospitality industry. What started as a team serving just three pioneering clients has grown into Nigeria's leading hospitality technology firm.

Over two and a half decades, we have earned recognition through multiple hospitality service awards in Nigeria and accolades from Micros Fidelio Inc., affirming our position as an industry pioneer committed to innovation and excellence.

Today, we power over 300 establishments across Nigeria — from independent boutique hotels and fine dining restaurants to international branded properties and fast-food chains — all with Oracle-powered technology.

Three Powerful Platforms for Hotels

Current-Link delivers the complete Oracle Hospitality suite — from property management to catering sales and cost control. All platforms integrate seamlessly with each other.

Core Platform

Oracle OPERA PMS

A full-featured property management system that enables hotels to deliver world-class guest service and increase operational efficiency. Used by thousands of properties globally, OPERA equips staff with tools for reservations, check-in/check-out, room inventory, guest accounting, and billing — all in one unified platform.

  • Comprehensive guest profile & preference management
  • Complex rate management including negotiated corporate rates
  • Over 360 standard reports plus custom reporting
  • Intelligent room allocation (least-occupied-first logic)
  • Automated booking rule enforcement
  • Cloud & on-premise deployment options
  • Integration with loyalty programs, leisure & financial systems
  • Multi-property chain management capability
Events & Revenue

Oracle OPERA Sales & Catering

A comprehensive catering and sales management application that integrates directly with Oracle OPERA PMS, enabling seamless data sharing between the hotel sales office and the property management system for maximum revenue generation from events and group business.

  • Account, contact & activity CRM for sales teams
  • Business blocks for group bookings, catering events & tour series
  • Sales representative dashboards with single-point access to key data
  • Function diary — graphical display of events by function space
  • Banquet posting: event charges transferred directly to OPERA billing
  • Potentials feature to define and track client value
  • Over 50 out-of-the-box reports and data queries
  • Flexible hotel-specific customization options
Cost Control

Oracle Hospitality Materials Control

A comprehensive procurement, inventory, and cost control system built specifically for hospitality. Provides real-time information on cost of sales, effective revenue, stock on hand, order proposals, and stocking requirements — all reviewable by outlet, property, or portfolio.

  • Real-time cost of sales and revenue analysis
  • Streamlined purchasing & supplier management
  • Direct PO transmission to suppliers (email, fax, B2B)
  • Paperless internal requisitions via web client
  • Purchase Manager: review & approve requests, combine multi-outlet orders
  • Flexible authorization workflows with electronic notifications
  • Mobile ordering, receiving, transfers & inventory counts
  • Multi-currency and multi-tax support
